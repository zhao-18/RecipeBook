\recipe{
Egg Muffin in a Mug
}[
%Optional initial instructions.
]

\begin{step}
%. . Notes
%Ingredients for this step
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
$\iquarter$ teaspoon baking powder
$\ieight$ teaspoon baking soda
$\ieight$ teaspoon salt
\method
%Step1
%\temp{350}
In a large microwave-safe mug mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt with a fork.
\end{step}

\begin{step}
%Ingredients for this step
2 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons vegetable oil or melted butter
2 tablespoons egg
1 tablespoon grated cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon scallions chopped
\method
%Step2
Add in the milk, oil, egg, cheese, and scallions and mix together until just combined.
\end{step}

\begin{step}
%. . Notes
%Ingredients for this step
1 small egg
\method
Make a well in the center of the batter with a spoon and crack in your egg into the center.
%Step3

Spoon the batter from the sides over the top of the egg.
%Step4 with no ingredients

Place in the microwave (1200W) and cook for 50 seconds- 1 minute 10 seconds. you will know when it is cooked because it will be firm to the touch on top. Always keep a close eye on your mug while in the microwave so it doesnâ€™t overflow or overcook.
%Step5 with no ingredients
\end{step}
